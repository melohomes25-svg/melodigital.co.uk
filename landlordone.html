Melo Digital Ltd App

LandlordOne

LandlordOne is a property record and management app designed for landlords who want a simple, practical way to stay on top of their portfolio.

The app is built to help users keep key information organised, including rent, expenses, compliance and property details, all in one place.

What It Does

Built for clarity and day-to-day use

Property Overview

Keep important property information together in a clear structure that is easy to review and update.

Rent Tracking

Record rent-related information so users can quickly see expected, received and outstanding amounts.

Compliance Reminders

Store and monitor important property compliance information, helping users keep track of key dates and obligations.

Expense Recording

Log expenses against a property to keep records organised and easier to review.
